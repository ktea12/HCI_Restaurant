About Us

Sushi originated as casual food served at food stalls in the Edo era. Sushi chefs would brush "nikiri soy sauce" and "nitsume (or tsume) sauce" on top on the sushi they made and place them in front of customers.

We believe conversing during the meal and sharing sake together is fundamental to it's origin and with only with Sake can we truly enjoy and bring out the umami flavor within sushi. Since 1969, Sushi + Sake maintains the tradition of Edo-style sushi and Sake with high standards.
